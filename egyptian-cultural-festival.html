Egyptian Cultural Festival

Event Details

Date: March 15-17, 2024

Location: Cairo, Egypt

The Egyptian Cultural Festival is a vibrant celebration of Egypt's rich history and diverse culture. Join us for a series of performances, workshops, and exhibitions that showcase traditional music, dance, and cuisine.

Activities Include:

  • Live Music and Dance Performances
  • Traditional Egyptian Cuisine Sampling
  • Art Exhibitions from Local Artists
  • Workshops on Egyptian Crafts

Tickets:

Tickets are available online or at the venue. Early bird discounts apply until February 1, 2024.
