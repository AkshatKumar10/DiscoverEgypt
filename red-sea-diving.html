The Red Sea

About

Diving:

The Red Sea is home to vibrant coral reefs, thousands of fish species, and famous shipwrecks. Some of the top diving sites include Ras Mohammed, Elphinstone, Dolphin Reef, and Rocky Island.

Beaches:

The Red Sea has many beautiful beaches, including those in Hurghada, Marsa Alam, and Sharm El Sheikh.

Resorts:

The Red Sea has many resorts, including high-end options in popular resort towns. The St. Regis Red Sea Resort on Ummahat Island has a spa, pool, kids club, and fitness center.

Other activities:

In addition to diving, visitors can snorkel, take boat trips, relax on the beach, and explore the desert.
